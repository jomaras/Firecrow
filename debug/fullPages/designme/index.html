Put down your dark glasses for a while, get rid of the black and white TV set and allow us to snatch you into the world of colours, shapes and joy of creating. Into the world of graphic design.

We are a small but professional web & graphic design studio based in Zvolen, Slovakia and we are focusing on pure and modern design. Our web presentations are characterized by intuitive user interface and furthermore, they meet the worldwide trends and all the international standards. We offer web & graphic design, xHTML and CSS template coding, Wordpress templates (design & code), flash banners, logotypes, business cards and other graphic services. We will be pleased to create the entire company identity for you.

What we offer

  • web design & development
  • logotype & corporate design
  • print design & DTP
  • flash banners & interactive
  • xHTML & css template coding
  • complete Wordpress templates
  • custom CMS
